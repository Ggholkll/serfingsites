Beginner Tips
Surfing is great, but it's not easy to learn. Moreover, when you start, you
never know how good you will be.
Choose an experienced instructor.
A surf instructor is a person who introduces you to the ocean, and it largely depends on him whether you are in for pleasure or disappointment.
Ride only on spots for beginners.
Different waves require different levels of training. If your level is minimal, you should not climb big sharp waves.
Take it easy.
Fuss is a bad helper, a good surfer should be calm. Do not rush into the waves, you have your whole life ahead of you to catch a bunch of waves!
